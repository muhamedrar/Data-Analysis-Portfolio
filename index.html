Canadian Salary Trends Analysis

Analysed salary data from Stack Overflow surveys in Canada, covering the years 2011 to 2023. The goal was to understand how salaries have changed over time, which industries and companies offer the highest pay, and how factors such as experience and location affect earnings. Created a dashboard with easy-to-read charts and graphs that display salary trends, helping people understand how different factors influence pay across Canada.
